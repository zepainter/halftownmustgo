The Two Row Wampum

The Two Row Wampum is a treaty initially established between the Haudenosaunee and Dutch settlers in 1613. Its principles were later extended to the fledgling United States. It commits to non-interference in one another's governance affairs while proceeding in relationships of peace and friendship.

The Great Law of Peace

The Great Law of Peace is essentially the constitution of the Haudenosaunee Confederacy.
